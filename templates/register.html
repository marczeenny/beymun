Dais Members

Be part of a committee's dais as chair, co-chair...

Application

Fill the following form.
Applications will then be reviewed by the competent team, and we will in turn get back to you.

Conference

The final conference is on the 12th and 13th of April 2025 at the campus of the American University of Beirut (location here)
Dais members are expected to be physically present on campus on these dates.
Chairing a committee requires also writing a Background Guide, correcting Position Papers... But these duties will be introduced to accepted dais applicants in a proper introductory meeting, which date will be communicated later on.

Requirements

Only university students or senior high school students are eligible to take part as DAIS in this year's BEYMUN conference.
However, high school students (grade 9 to 12) as well as university students can take part as delegates (see below).
Prior MUN experience is required, while prior MUN chairing experience is a plus.

Delegates

Register now and take your seat at the debate!

Registration

Delegates may register for the conference by filling the form at mun.org.lb{{ url_for('delegate_regist') }}. Registration closes on the 14th of March 2025.

Registration fees:
  • National Delegates: USD 30
  • International delegates: USD 60

Note: Registration for delegates opens on Thursday, February 13th

Payment

Payment can be made through the Wish app by searching for "Beymun AUB", or by looking for "Beymun AUB" in the "Sports & Events" section.
Note: Make sure you are in the USD section of Wish.

We will soon release an update regarding alternative methods of payment, concerning international delegates, and delegates who can not use the Wish app.
As always, for any inquiry, do not hesitate to contact us at beymun@aub.edu.lb.
