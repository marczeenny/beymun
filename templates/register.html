International Delegates

For this year's edition, we have chosen the mymun platform for international delegates and delegations. The BEYMUN 2025 page is accessible at mun.org.lb/int.
The fees for international delegates amount to USD 60 to be paid through WesternUnion. You will receive (by email) the payment coordinates once you register on MyMun.

National Delegates

Delegates may register for the conference by filling the form at mun.org.lb{{ url_for('delegate_regist') }}. Registration closes on the 7th of April 2025.
Note: Payment of the registration fee should be made in anticipation of filling the registration form, as the Whish receipt is to be attached.

Payment

Fees for national delegates: USD 30 (USD 20 for AUB Students)
Payment can be made either through the Whish app or the BobFinance app by searching for "Beymun AUB". Make sure you are in the USD section if you're using Whish.
Regarding delegates who do not have a Whish or BobFinance account, you can always pay at any of their physical stores throughout Lebanon, by simply indicating BEYMUN AUB.
As always, for any inquiry, do not hesitate to contact us at beymun@aub.edu.lb.
Please note that the registration fee is NOT refundable.

Delegations

In addition to each delegate filling the form mentionned above, delegates can choose to represent their school by letting an advisor fill the form at mun.org.lb{{ url_for('skl_regist') }}.
Advisors will have to also upload a spreadsheet containing the first, middle, and last names of the delegation's members, each in a separate column.
There is NO restriction on the number of delegates per delegation, however the maximum number of Advisors per delegation is two, with a main one and a secondary one.

Best of luck!
